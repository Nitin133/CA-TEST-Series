Question 3 : What is difference between Detailed Test Series, unscheduled test series and Full Syllabus Test Series. Do detailed Test Series include full syllabus Tests?
Detailed Test Series (Scheduled test series), papers will be provided on scheduled dates. Student can attempt papers till 31st October or 30th April. Means, student can postpone the test but pre-pone is not allowed. There will be 5 chapterwise and 1 Full test for each subject.

Unscheduled Test Series, papers will be provided at once immediately after registration. Student can attempt at any time till 31st October or 30th April. There will be 5 chapterwise and 1 Full test for each subject.

Full Syllabus Test Series; there will be only 2 full syllabus test papers for each subject. No chapterwise test. Papers will be provided on scheduled dates
