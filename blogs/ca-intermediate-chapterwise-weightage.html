In this field, the most reputed and influential career line is known to be a Chartered Accountant (CA). Usually, Chartered Accountancy is considered as a vital, rewarding and challenging profession. Since you have cleared the first level; CA Foundation with flying colors, now you can have your complete focus on the next level in this field. 

 

CA Intermediate is the second level in the CA line that you need to clear to move forward to the third level. Each level is more difficult than earlier so buckle up to study hard and smart. Aspiring individuals who have registered for the CA Intermediate course are advised to connect with CA Test Series and get on with their preparation before it’s too late.

Check Out the CA Intermediate Syllabus

 

  • The syllabus of CA Intermediate has undergone various changes. Within the revised scheme of education initiated by ICAI, there has been a lot of alterations and up gradations in the syllabus of the CA Intermediate level.

 

  • Earlier it was known as CA IPCC that is now renamed as CA Intermediate. Under the old system, there are 7 papers in total, now there are 8 papers. The total marks of the exam were 700, whereas now, it is 800.

 

  • Once you have gone through the complete syllabus, it is time for you to get ready to start with the preparation and for that, you need to first your study timetable and schedule. You can plan it as per the CA Test Series Schedule; however, ensure to follow it with the utmost attention and dedication if you wish to score well at this level.

 

 

Study the CA Intermediate Exam Pattern

 

  • The Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI) introduced the new exam pattern for CA Intermediate level. The exams will be conducted offline just like any other exam. Individuals who are preparing to give the CA Intermediate level exams must study the exam pattern of their respective year in order to understand the changes in both old and new schemes along with several aspects related to the question paper.

 

  • As per the CA Intermediate exam pattern, 70% of the paper will hold subjective, long questions and the remaining 30% consists of MCQs. The exam pattern of this exam has got 8 papers distributed into two groups. Hence, there are four papers in each group that is as per your choice to appear for once at a time.

 

  • The exam for the CA Intermediate Group 1 will be first and then after a few days, you will have to give the exam for Group 2. However, it totally depends on your preparation and ability on how you want to give the exams. According to the latest exam pattern, students must score a minimum of 50% overall and 40% each paper to qualify the CA Intermediate level.

 

If you have already applied for this year’s session, you should go through the CA Intermediate exam pattern so that there is no room for doubt and confusion when your exams are around the corner. With a reliable and trusted CA Inter Test Series, you will be able to manage your study plan and professional’s assistance in order to acquire great rank in this level.
