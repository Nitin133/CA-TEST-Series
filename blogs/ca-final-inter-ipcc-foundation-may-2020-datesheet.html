CA FINAL, Date sheet for Nov 2020 Exams

This notification is for those students, who are eagerly waiting for examination update for May 2020 attempt. Institute of Charted Accountant of India announced the date sheet for May 2020 attempt that earlier scheduled from 19th June to 4th July. It had also rescheduled from 29 July 9, 2020 to 16 August 2020 and the said examinations will now be held in Nov 2020 attempt as per the announcement of ICAI.

Owing to spread of Covid-19, the ICAI exams may 2020 cycle has been merged with Nov 2020 attempt. However, these exams are rescheduled for all India bases and abroad.

ICAI also announced the timing for Nov 2020 exams in the notification for Nov 2020 attempt. The exams will be conducted in the afternoon between 2:00 to 5:00 PM (except elective papers). You should note down that there is no change in the Examination centres for CA Final Old and new course students. The time duration for elective paper is of 4 Hours, all other examinations are of 2 hours.

Students, you know very well a time- table plays a virtual role in the successful achievements. A proper time- table is a key factor and it should be achievable. There is era of smart work rather than hard work. No doubt hard work is also required but it should be done smartly. So follow the best strategy to revise the whole over syllabus of CA Final course.

We have made the expected time schedule for your preparation. You can prepare your time table accordingly.

 

CA Foundation Date sheet for Nov 2020 Exams

ICAI (Institute of Charted Accountant Of India) has announced that Exams of CA Foundation May 2020 will be held in the month of November 2020.

Owing to Covid-19 and extensive of lockdown period ICAI has postponed the date of exams. Now exams will be held in the month of November that was scheduled on 11th May 2020 to 17th May 2020. In view of this pandemic situation May 2020 exams have been cancelled and merged with Nov 2020 attempt.

Those students who has already submitted their application form for May 2020 attempt, have given an option to correct their application forms, while making a fresh application form for CA Foundation Nov, 2020 attempt. However these application forms are available only in online mode at icaiexam.icai.org.

 Only registered students with the Board Of Studies (BOS) can be filled this examination forms. In this form you have to select the examination centre, and pay the required proceeds for the application fee. Same ID or password is used for all services.

Fees for Indian candidates is of Rs. 1500 and For foreigners is US$325 except Kathmandu Centre. However the fee for Nepal (Khatmandu) centres is Rs. 2200.They can pay the proceeds though various modes like Credit card, Debit card, Net banking , BHIM UPI and Ropay Cards etc.

Your one time registration is evergreen process and it can be done whole over the year. The registration fee and exam fee both are different. However exam fee is varying according to the exam centre.

For more updates relating to Nov 2020 Examinations student should stay in touch with the ICAI’s website that is www.icai.org.

 

Although Exam centers and exam timing i.e. (For Paper-1 and Paper 2 :- 2 p.m. to 5 p.m and For Paper 3 and Paper 4 2pm. To 4 p.m.)  have not changed but examination Date Sheet Changed.

 

The time duration of exams are:-

For Paper First and Second is 2 Hours. For Paper Third and Fourth is of 3 Hours.

 

CA INTER, Date sheet for Nov 2020 Exams

 

The Institute of Charted Accountant of India rescheduled the examination date sheet, CA Intermediate/IPCC exams will now be held in Nov 2020. May 2020 exam cycle has merged with Nov 2020 exam cycle. However, these exams have been postponed for all Indian and non residents citizens.

Student should keep touch with the latest announcement by the ICAI. ICAI has announced that timetable of exams of May 2020 attempt has changed for both Group I and Group II. This time table issue plays a major role for all CA students for studies. With this date sheet they can make their own time table.

Exam will be conducted in noon session from 2 to 5 pm. with time duration of 3 Hours. However examination centres and exam timing have not changed. Earlier the ICAI announced the time table in respect of July 2020 for CA foundation, Intermediate/IPCC and Final for both old and new, now going to be held in Nov 2020.

Now you have time for the preparation. You can make a perfect time table accordingly as per your convenience but time table is must to achieved desired results. Stay calm and stay home and focus on your studies. You should solve the Mock tests and Previous CA Inter/IPCC papers to test yourself. You can evaluate your performance before facing failure in the final exams.

How to prepare for November 2020 Exams

Ans – From day one prepare like if you have exam right next day. No one will come to help you, your planning, execution and Feedback should be very good. If you are thinking of studying for 14 to 16 hours, trust me your will not be able to grasp much content because after some time, your brain stops accepting new concept and you are just reading the same words again and again.

Your portfolio of revision should comprise of certain thinks like practice of questions, written practice, solving mcq, writing test papers, reading new points, review the test papers thoroughly, writing synopsis of theory questions etc. The level of ICAI exams are shifting to totally conceptual based.
