CA Final Exam Form May 2020

Now that both CA Intermediate level classes have been cleared, it's time to focus on the next step in the Chartered Accountancy course. You need to register by submitting the application for the CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams to give CA Final exams. Of the thousands of individuals appearing for CA Intermediate exams, in one attempt only a few students will clear both stages. If you’re looking for professional guidance and assistance, CA Test Series is the perfect option for you to choose among many.

 

Submit the Form on Time

If you have cleared both levels in one attempt, instantly look for the last late date of the exam form submission and register before it is too late. Make sure to check all the important deadlines and dates related to CA Final exams and set you schedule accordingly.

All the interested candidates applying for this course through online method need to pay a certain amount of fees for completing the whole process of registration. Once you are done with submitting your exam form, you can focus on making your study plan and set your schedule as per the CA Test Series Schedule.

 

Process of Submitting the CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams Application

Make sure to follow these prime steps when submitting the CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams:

  • STEP: 1 Login to Your Account

For submitting the CA Final online exam form, the first step is to login to your account on ICAI.org. Though, if you don’t have an account on the main website of ICAI, then you first need to create an account on it. The new account will be authenticated with your basic details such as student registration number, email id, date of birth, and phone number.

It is suggested by the experts to double check all the filled up information to avoid any errors. Fill up the latest email id of which you remember the password as you’ll get your application form sent on that email and mobile number.

  • STEP: 2 Fill Exam Form & Pay Fees Online

Right after properly creating your account, login with your username and the password mailed to your email. Fill up all the asked details in the CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams correctly, confirm all the specific information displayed in the confirmation page and lastly pay the fees utilizing any Master/Visa debit or credit card.

  • STEP: 3 Cross-check the Photo & Sign is Printed on the Form

If you have your photo and signature already printed on the form, then you don’t need to sign and send the print-out of the exam form to ICAI. Also, you only have to fill up the online form and pay the desired fees. In other cases like either your sign and photo is not printed on the CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams, then you have to take a print out, affix photo and sign it. Finally, send the form to the main office of ICAI. 

After you’re done with the filling of CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams, focus on finding the Best test series for CA final exams and start preparing with complete attention and dedication. If you do so, nothing will stop you from clearing this level with great rank and flying colors.

Get the Admit Card on Time

The admit card for CA Final exams are mostly issued by ICAI, 21 days before the beginning of the examination. You will receive your admit card on the given address while filling up the exam form. However, it can also be downloaded online if you want. Remember, you can’t sit for final exams without your admit card.

 

Problems in Submission of CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams

ICAI ensures that the complete procedure for online submission of CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams is error-free. Though, the possibility of minor errors is still possible due to different reasons like slow internet connection, probably issues experienced during the payment process or various other errors.

In case, you experience such errors and are not able to pay the fees online or the fees has been paid but you haven’t received the PDF, or any other reasons, don’t worry and don’t panic. Simply, pay the fees online once more and the PDF would be created successfully. Don’t forget to send an email to ICAI explaining that you have paid the fees twice and the excess fees would be refunded later on.

 

After CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams

Once the individuals have successfully filled the CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams, you can sole focus on preparing and studying for your exams that are just around the corner. Keep up with your schedule and follow it with complete dedication. Also, ensure to practice ICAI mock test papers CA final for a better understanding of your concepts and preparation.

Practicing mock test papers and revision test papers, you will understand your weaknesses and strengths. You will know on what topics to focus and what topics don’t need anymore practice. Create your own timetable instead of following someone else’s as your capability and learning ability is different from others.

Find the reliable CA Final Mock Test Series and get on with taking your test before the actual exams. Make sure to focus on the points and concepts that you find difficult to do during your mock tests in order to clear them for the final exams. It is a great of practicing for exams and will surely lead you to successfully clearing this level as well.

Where do I send my CA exam application form?

Ans- The CA Final / IPCC Exam Form submitted electronically will be sent to the Indian Institute of Chartered Accountants Additional Secretary (Exams), ICAI Bhawan, Indraprastha Marg, New Delhi–140002. The CA IPCC / CA Final Exam Admit Cards are usually given by ICAI 21 days prior to the start of the exams.

 

Do we need to send exam form to ICAI?

Ans-  Students whose photographs and signatures are not in the examination directory, i.e. those whose photographs and signatures are not shown on the screen, must print out the examination form, attach their photographs and signatures at the designated location, obtain the form certified by a Chartered Accountant or Gazetted Officer or Head of an Educational Institution and send the same.

 

When can I apply for CA final registration?

Ans- Until submitting the CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams, a student may apply for CA Final Registration at any time. The preparation of the CA Final Registration Form usually takes ICAI a few months and therefore we allow students to apply for CA Final Registration at least 3 months before submitting the CA Final Exam Form for May 2020 ICAI Exams.

 

Who can attest CA final examination form?

Ans- The CA Final Exam Form For May 2020 ICAI Exams has to be attested by a member of the Institute or you’re Principal in the case of Final candidates. However in the case of Intermediate (IPC) Exam candidates, it may be got attested by a Gazetted Officer also.

 

What p1 p3 p3. P8 stands for in CA intermediate exam form for availing exemption

Ans- There is no exemption process and the exemption is automatically granted to CA Intermediate CA / IPCC and ICAI's final students and the same would be stated on the students ' mark-sheet. Once an exemption is granted, the applicant will not be eligible for any other exemption in that category until the exemption given to him in that group has already been exhausted.
