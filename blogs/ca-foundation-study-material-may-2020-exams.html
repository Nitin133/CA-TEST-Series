Step 1: Visit ICAI's official website: icai.org

Step 2: A page with all 4 listed papers will appear. Tap any of the four articles.

Step 3: A new page will appear, now click on "Review Content"

Step 4: chapter topics will appear. To obtain the study material as PDF, click on any subject.

 

How To Get CA Foundation Books From ICAI?

Ans- Follow the steps for CA Foundation Books as follows: Step 1: Visit ICAI's official website: icai-cds.org.

Step 2: From here, registered users can use their identification number and date of birth to click on "Sign In" from the top left corner. On the other hand, in order to purchase the books, guest users must register themselves by clicking the "Sign Up" button.

Step 3: A new page appears where you see in one row' Students Books' and in the next one ' Latest Items.' Tap "Open Everything" in the section called "Students Books."

Step 5: In the filter box of the list, pick "Foundation" and then choose the language–English or Hindi.

Phase 6: A list of books included in the package of the foundation report. Select "Package for the Study Foundation."

Step 7: A new page opens with the kit's list of books. Choose the books and put them in your cart. Check out the things in your cart.

Phase 8: The payment and order must be made by non-registered users. Registered users can place an order directly. Delivery usually takes 14 working days and can be tracked using the AWB number via AWB monitoring. The AWB number that appears when you put the order should be noted.

What are CA Foundation's passing marks?

Ans-Candidates must score 40 marks in each subject of the CA Foundation and 50% overall in order to clear the CA Foundation.

 

Is there a derogatory branding on the CA Foundation?

Ans-For each incorrect response, the objective questions (papers 3 and 4) will bear a negative 1⁄4 mark mark.

 

Is a calculator included in the review of CA Foundation?

Ans-Yes. ICAI allows candidates in the examination hall to use calculators. With up to 6 functions, 14 digits and up to two memories, the calculators should be of any type.

 

When are the CA Foundation exams going to take place in May 2020?

Ans-The May 2020 examination of the CA Foundation, consisting of four papers, will take place on May 9, 14, 13 and 15 (temporary).
