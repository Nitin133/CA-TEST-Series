To become a Chartered Accountant in India is not an easy task. A person has to prove himself at various steps to become a CA. There are various stages or levels that an individual has to pass to become a Chartered Accountant. Firstly, an aspirant has to pass the first level of exam, to give the CA Inter exam. If the student has cleared the CA Inter exam, then he becomes eligible to give the CA Final exam. A CA Aspirant does not only have to pass the exams of the CA Final to become a CA but also he has to complete a compulsory training of 3 years in a Chartered Accounting Firm, to become a CA. It means that a student has to study as well as to gain practical exposure in the industry to become a CA. This makes the process even more exciting and challenging for the students.

Giving the CA Final exam is the most difficult step which a student has to pass to become a CA. A student can pass this exam, only if he is completely dedicated and focused to pass this exam. If a student follows the right approach with the right practice, then only he will be able to crack this exam. The CA Final Exam is divided into two groups, and there are 4 exams that a student has to pass in both of these groups. Some of the subjects that the student as to study while preparing for the CA Final exam are Risk Management, Strategic Cost Management, and Indirect Tax Laws. All these subjects test the students on their ability to become a good Chartered Accountant.

 

The most important thing that comes in the preparation part of becoming a CA is the right approach to practice. One of the best ways that you can use to practice for your exams is by solving the Past Years' RTP of CA Final Year. Revision Test Papers are simply one of the best methods to practise various concepts of our CA Final Preparation. When a student tries to work on these Revision Test Papers, it helps him to see what kind of questions can be asked in the main exam. These revision test papers are created by the expert teachers and Chartered Accountants. These test papers include all the important topics that a student must cover before sitting for the main exam. 

 

Why you should not miss these Revision Test Papers?

 

1: When you solve a revision test paper you get to know what are the topics that you have a good command on, and what are the topics that you need to work on again. It helps you to build confidence in solving the questions, which can prove out to be extremely helpful for you in the long run. 

 

2: These Past Year RTPs of CA Final exam include a lot of important questions. These questions are based on the topics that have previously come in the exams, and also there are a few questions that have already been asked in the previous years. It gives a student a complete idea about the questions that he has to work on, to pass the CA Final exam.

 

3: When a CA Aspirant solves these Revision Test Papers, he gets the hold of time properly. A student can simply keep a track of the time that he is taking to solve the questions. The main focus of the students should be to reduce the time of solving the questions with each passing revision test paper. 

 

4: Solving more and more revision test papers can contribute a lot in helping you to gain accuracy in solving the questions in the exam. A lot of times, students know how to solve the question, but they end up solving it incorrectly. By attempting more Past Year RTPs of CA Final exam, a student can gradually improve his accuracy in solving these questions. It is because the more you practice the better you become.

 

5: If you are solving these revision test papers, then you get a chance to analyze your performance. The more you analyze your performance, the more you can understand the need for practice in your CA Final Exam Preparation. You can analyze your methods, concepts and understanding of the topic after doing every single revision test paper.

 

A lot of CAs have followed the same path of solving more and more Revision Test Papers during their preparation years. According to them, this Past Year RTPs of CA Final has helped them to pass their CA Final exam. They proved to be helpful for them, and now they can prove out to be helpful for you as well. 

 

Quickly start solving these revision test papers and see your performance going up rapidly.

 

 

Best Wishes!! To all CA final students, who are appearing in this Nov, 2020 attempt.
