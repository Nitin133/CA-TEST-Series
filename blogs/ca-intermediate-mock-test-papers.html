CA Intermediate All Mock Test Papers in Pdf

To be a chartered accountant in India, everyone has to follow a set pattern. There are different exams that all the candidates appear for, and if they pass all of these exams, they become a chartered accountant. Firstly, the students have to pass the CA Foundation exam, also known as CPT exam. It is the entry-level exam for all the CA aspirants. The difficulty level of this exam is not too tough as it only checks the basic understanding of the students. Once the student has passed this CA Foundation exam, he has to appear for the CA Intermediate or CA Inter exam.

Institute of Charted Accountants of India conducts two test series before happening on exams. These Mock test papers are real exam based papers, but the marks of these mock test papers are not counted with the final result. Hence, these mock test papers are conducted for the preparation of CA students, so that they can get to the latest exam pattern with the new marking scheme. The suggested answers are also released by ICAI to enhance the presentation skills in a student. The student can easily know a proper way to answer a relevant question.

If you have revised your syllabus or you are revising, you cannot consider it as an ending process, because without attempting these Mock Test papers your preparation is incomplete. You must attempt these papers for better results. You can compare your actual performance with standard performance, after attempting these mock test papers. However, after completion of Mock test series special counseling sessions are also held, in which students can post their doubts, at least 45 hours in advance before the scheduled dates of counseling.

CA Intermediate Exam

The CA Intermediate Exam is the second level of exam, which the CA aspirants have to appear for. This exam acts as one of the most important stages in the journey of CA Aspirant. The CA Inter exam is divided into two groups. There are 8 subjects that a student has to study in these groups. These subjects are as follows:

 

Group 1-

Paper 1: Accounting

Paper 2: Corporate Laws and Other Laws

Paper 3: Cost and Management Accounting

Paper 4: Taxation

 

Group 2-

Paper 5: Advanced accounting

Paper 6: Auditing and Assurance

Paper 7: Enterprise Information Systems and Strategic Management

Paper 8: Financial Management and Economics for Finance

 

All of these papers are of 100 marks each. Therefore, it becomes very important for all the students appearing for these exams to be well prepared for the exam to pass these exams. All of these exams are of quite a high difficulty level, and hence it requires a lot of practice and hard work to pass these exams. A student has to follow a correct pattern to prepare to pass these exams. 

 

Importance of Mock Tests to prepare for CA Inter Exam

 

Mock tests are one of the best ways to prepare for any exam that you are giving. These mock tests help the students to prepare for the exam according to the exam pattern. Therefore, students preparing for the CA Inter Exam must give CA Inter Mock Test Series to score well in their exams. Let us look at some points that why should opt for a test series to pass your CA Inter Exam.

 

1: ICAI has introduced some changes in the exam pattern for the CA Inter exams. The CA Inter Mock Test Series will provide you with mock papers designed the updated format, and hence you will be getting the practice on the latest format of the question paper. It will help you to boost your confidence and will prepare you well for your main exam.

2: Mock tests act as a mirror to your preparation. When you give a mock test, you get to realize that what your strong and weak points are. You get to know the topics on which you have a good command and the topics on which you need to devote more time. It helps you to analyze your preparation level in the most appropriate way.

 

3: These CA Inter Mock Test Series Free are made by the experts, who have a great experience in preparing students for these CA exams. Hence, you get to see a question paper which would be very close to the actual level of the question paper that you will get in your exam. There are chances that you might even get some same questions in your main exam which were there in these mock tests.

 

4: When you prepare for your exam, you prepare and practice chapter-wise. But in your main exam, you won't get the questions in the same order. Therefore, these mock tests help the students to prepare for the real exam, where all the topics are jumbled up, and you have to identify that question is from which topic.

 

5: You get to learn time management which is the most important aspect of the CA Inter exam. You get to prepare in the real-time atmosphere. By giving more and more mock tests, you gradually build up your speed and get a healthy idea about the exam.

 

The best part about these CA Inter Exam Mock Tests Series is that you can easily access them and that too without paying for them as they are free of cost. All you need to do is that you have to prepare all the topics to the best of your ability and then you have to come and give a mock test. It will let you know the level of your preparation and will help you to boost your confidence.

 

Prepare well, and try to give as many mock tests as you can. It will help you to crack your CA Inter Exam easily by building a foundation that will help you to throughout your CA journey.
