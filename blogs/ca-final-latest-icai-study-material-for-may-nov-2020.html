Get involved in Subject wise preparation with CA final study material May 2020

 

Approach- While preparing for the CA final you must analyze the syllabus and understand which part of the syllabus is more important that will bring more questions in CA finals. Ask your teachers and seniors and try to know on your own which chapters should be prepared first and given more time. Check the amendments that happened in the syllabus and use study material to analyze which part has the maximum probability to come in CA final exam.

 

Syllabus- CA aspirants and students start studying the chapters and preparing syllabus before months of exam.  Being an active student you must go through ICAI mock test papers, reference books, previous year papers, and study material to find out what is important and should be on your priority. After complete research, they make a plan and start preparing for the exam.

 

Make Time Table- Try to make a time table subject wise since you will have to finish the one subject by completely focusing on it. After completing the first subject then you can jump on to the next one. This way you will be able to make notes of highlights along with the study and get the syllabus done systematically. Also, make sure to prepare theory and practical subjects equally with a positive approach.

 

Time Allotment- To prepare for the CA Final exam, initially you should begin the preparation by giving at least 3-4 hours a day but as you get closer to the time of the exams start allotting 8-10 hours a day for the preparation. Work according to the time table that you have made and do not skip the preparation time. If you have kept it for preparation then make sure you are only studying at that time.

 

 

Revision is an essential part of your CA final preparation

 

When to start- Revision of the CA final exam should be started at least 2-3 months before the exams. Start the revision of the exam by taking help from your notes you have prepared or ICAI mock test papers that help you analyze your preparation and revise them if you are lacking at some point. Take a quiet place for preparation from the CA final study material and understand your weaknesses and strength. Use it appropriately to clear the CA final exam.

 

Strategy- Make sure that your CA final syllabus has already been prepared before you start the revision. Go through the highlighted topics and questions instead of re studying the chapters in-depth again. If you do this you might forget the important questions and parts to revise. This is the time you are revising the last time before the exams so, do it properly.

 

Other important tips to prepare well from the CA final study material

 

  • Being the CA aspirant you might have the feeling to grasp as much as you can but you should also understand that you cannot finish everything until you are a genius. Students rush for every popular book that promises to give you the best result this will only leave you confused about the concepts and which to start. This is why it is important that you refer to the CA final study material and CA Final Test Series to study. But make sure to prepare for the exam without affecting your health.

 

  • Some people say coaching is important and some say that self-study is important for cracking the CA final exam. But in fact, the coaching and self-study are equally important for the preparation. You learn new concepts in coaching and systematical study in the classroom whereas self-study is also essential to grasp the syllabus with your own speed and understanding.

 

  • Try to go through the practice manuals since every student does that before the AC final exams. But the question is when to start it? You should go through the ICAI Test Series once during the time of the revision. These are a great source of important questions and have repeated questions that come in exams CA final exam. So, go through them well and prepare.

 

Tips to be implemented in CA final exam

 

  • Make sure to use the reading time given by ICAI is properly used. Try to prioritize the questions in that time and attempt according to that structure.

 

  • The presentation of the answer is very important in the exams since this is the CA final exam. So, make sure your answers are relevant in the exam rather than being lengthy and irrelevant.

 

  • Make sure your answers are up to the point and neatly written since this enables the examiner to read them easily and leaves a good impact on them.

 

We hope these tips will be helpful for you in preparing the CA Final exam and score well. CA Test Series will help you choose the right study material and crack the toughest exam. So, make sure you are going systematically while preparing for the exam.

FAQ's

Q : How to apply in ca course in india? ?
A : You have to follow a complete process from CA Foundation or direct entry. Register on ICAI.ORG
Q : What are CA final subjects? ?
A : Paper 1: Financial Reporting [100 Marks] Paper 2: Strategic Financial Management [100 Marks] Paper 3: Advanced Auditing and Professional Ethics [100 Marks] Paper 4: Corporate and Allied Laws [100 Marks] Section A: Company Law [70 Marks] Section B: Allied Laws [30 Marks]
Q : How many attempts we can give for becoming CA? ?
A : You can give any number of attempts with subsequent registrations. Registration is done for 5 years, in that period you can give 10 attempts
Q : Are the modifications in ca final syllabus applicable for old course also? ?
A : Various changes are there in CA Final new course material as compared to old course. Check ICAI latest study material.
Q : Is there any course compulsory to become ca? ?
A : Practical Training (3 Years) is a mandatory part of CA course duration. Candidates become eligible to register for Article ship training once they passed either single or both groups of CA Intermediate.
Q : How do I register for ca final. And I was not able to find in icai website? ?
A : CA Final New Course Registration Procedure: Step 1: Register yourself on icai.org Step 2: Create your ID on their website and Fill registration form from your account.
Q : How do I switch from old syllabus to new syllabus ICAI? ?
A : Step by step procedure to switch over from CA old syllabus to CA new syllabus. Step 1 : Go to ICAI.org Step 2 : Login to your account with Email and password. Step 3 : Once login, you can see an application form to change youâ€™re the course which you have to fill all your details. Step 4 : Certain documents like school leaving certificate, signature needs to upload in scanned copy. Step 5 : Make online payment and then download application form generated by the system. At last submit application form to ICAI regional office.
Q : Time limit for registering CA final course? ?
A : Time limit to register for CA Final depends on the two cases:- If you cleared both groups of CA Inter or IPCC at once then you have to register along with articleship only. If you cleared one by one then you have to register atleast 6 months prior to the attempt.
Q : What is the last date for registration for CA Finals for May 2020? ?
A : You should register atleast 6 month prior to May 2020 and complete 30 months of training
